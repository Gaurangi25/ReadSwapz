Help Center

Your guide to using ReadSwapz

What is ReadSwapz?

ReadSwapz is an online platform where you can rent, buy, or donate books of all genres. It brings together book lovers who can share books in a simple and easy way.

How does ReadSwapz work?

Browse through a wide range of books, select the ones you want to borrow or rent, and enjoy reading! You can also donate your books to others in the community.

How can I donate books?

Simply sign up, select the books you want to donate, and list them on the platform for others to borrow.

Is there a fee for renting books?

The fees for renting books vary based on the book and the duration. You can see the rental price on each book's page.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support via the 'Contact Us' section on our website or through our email support.
